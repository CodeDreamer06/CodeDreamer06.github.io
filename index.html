§ 2 Reading & Interests

I read philosophy and psychology to understand myself and the world a little better. Much of that comes from thinking about meaning, mortality, and how to make good use of limited time. My current interests include emotional intelligence, codependency, childhood’s imprint on personality, dream analysis, and frameworks for self-understanding.

I also enjoy chess, Greek mythology, and learning about how people structure deep work. I’ve played the piano for as long as I can remember; I'm trying to write novels, though that's very much a work in progress.

I like optimising small things: keyboard shortcuts, playback speed, how a workspace is laid out. Partly out of curiosity, partly because my spine reminds me to be kinder to my body.
